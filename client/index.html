Expo The annual Festival Eritrea

Eritrea has many Christian holidays, Muslim holiday and National holidays

Some of the national holidays are:

Festival Eritrea Expo(one week) July 29th

Independence Day(one week) May 24th

Martyrs Day June 20th

Cuisine Eritrean Restaurants Serve both traditional and Italian foods

The main traditional food in Eritrean cuisine is tsebhi (stew), served with injera (flatbread made from teff, wheat, or sorghum

Italian dishes are also very popular in Eritrea. Most restaurants serve lasagna, spaghetti and other pasta's. There are various Pizza restaurants in Asmara.

Culture

Eritrea has 9 ethinic groups. Ethnic distinctions are evident in the dances and the kinds of music that each group produces

Religion is a mix of many beliefs,majority of the population practices Christian or Muslim faith.

One of the most interesting aspects of the Eritrean culture is the traditional coffee ceremony
