Hotels In Asmara

Asmara Palace

The Asmara Palace Hotel (Airport Road) was constructed in 1999. It is a high-end hotel with marble bathrooms, shops, conference halls, swimming pool, a tennis court and other sports and fitness facilities. The hotel is just 1 km from Asmara International Airport, near the Expo.

Alla Scala Hotel

The Alla Scala Hotel is a modern four star hotel in Asmara Eritrea. The hotel started its operations on May 24 2000. The hotel is about 3km. from Asmara International Airport, near the Expo and not far from downtown Asmara.

Lion Hotel

The Lion Hotel is one of the most modern hotels in Asmara and features: 28 first class suites, Restaurant serving traditional and international dishes, Terraces for refreshment and relaxation Spacious hall for reception and wedding feasts or band playing.

Africa Pension

A former mayoral villa with colonial charm and a beautiful garden. Build in the 20's, the Africa Pension was used as a High Court during the period of Ethiopian occupation. During the last 20 years before liberation, it was used as a bar and by the Ethiopian Navy.
